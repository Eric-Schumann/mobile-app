Flexbox

January 6 ⋅ 6:00 PM

Let's kickoff 2016 by learning about one of the best things to happen to css in a long time - Flexbox.

Flexbox allows us to accomplish layouts in a matter of seconds which would generally be much more time consuming, verbose, and sometimes even impossible(without the use of Javascript) with the regular ole' block layout.

This will be almost entirely a front-end driven session, however, if you are more of a back-end dev who is instantly annoyed with css concepts like floats, then you will definitely want to attend this session!
